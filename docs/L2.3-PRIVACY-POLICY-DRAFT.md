## 3. Output — Complete Privacy Policy

### 1. Introduction

**Purpose.** This Privacy Policy explains how **WiseWave Limited** collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data when you use the Tirvea platform, including our websites, mobile applications, and related services (together, the **"Service"**). It also explains your rights and how to exercise them.

**Our commitment.** We process personal data in accordance with the principles of lawfulness, fairness, and transparency; purpose limitation; data minimisation; accuracy; storage limitation; integrity and confidentiality; and accountability. Tirvea is an adults-only (18+) dating service, so we take particular care with the sensitive nature of the data involved.

**How to read this Policy.** Defined terms are set out in §2. Related policies — such as the Biometric Information Policy (`/legal/biometric-data`) and the Data Retention Policy (`/legal/data-retention`) — are referenced where relevant and are part of how we describe our processing; we do not repeat their detail here.

### 2. Definitions

In this Policy, the following terms have the meanings given. Data-protection terms not defined here take their meaning from the GDPR.

- **"Biometric Data"** — personal data resulting from specific technical processing relating to facial characteristics that allows or confirms unique identification (GDPR Article 9). On the Service it arises only from Photo Verification (§§11–12).
- **"Controller"** — the person who determines the purposes and means of processing personal data. The Controller is WiseWave Limited (§4).
- **"GDPR"** — Regulation (EU) 2016/679, and, where applicable, the UK GDPR.
- **"Personal Data"** — any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
- **"Processing"** — any operation performed on Personal Data, whether automated or not.
- **"Processor"** — a person who processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller (for example, AWS, Stripe, and Supabase, as described in §§34–38).
- **"Service"** — the Tirvea platform, as defined in §1.
- **"Special Category Data"** — the categories of Personal Data listed in GDPR Article 9, including Biometric Data used for unique identification.
- **"Tirvea"** — the brand and platform name for the Service.
- **"User"**, **"you"**, or **"your"** — any natural person who uses the Service.
- **"WiseWave Limited"**, **"we"**, **"us"**, or **"our"** — the Controller identified in §4.

- **Purpose.** To create and secure your Account and provide the Service.
- **Data categories.** Identifiers used to register and authenticate — such as phone number and/or email address, account identifiers, authentication events, age-confirmation (18+) records, and the versions of the Terms, Privacy Policy, and Community Guidelines you accepted.
- **Legal basis.** Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)); compliance with legal obligations for age assurance and record-keeping (Art. 6(1)(c)); and our legitimate interests in account security (Art. 6(1)(f)).
- **Recipients.** Our authentication and database Processor (Supabase, §37) and other Processors as needed to operate the Service.
- **Retention.** For the life of the Account and then as described in the Data Retention Policy (`/legal/data-retention`), including erasure within 30 days of deletion subject to legal or safety holds.
- **Your rights.** Access, correction, deletion, and the other rights in §§42–48.

### 9. Profile Information

- **Purpose.** To create your dating profile and enable Matching and Discovery.
- **Data categories.** Information you choose to add — such as display name, photographs, prompts, biographical details, and preferences. Some details you add may be sensitive (for example, information revealing aspects of your personal life); you choose what to include.
- **Legal basis.** Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) for core profile functionality; **explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(e)/(a))** where you voluntarily make Special Category Data public through your profile. **[Legal review required before publication — confirm the Article 9 basis for user-published sensitive profile details, including the "manifestly made public" analysis.]**
- **Recipients.** Other users, to the extent your profile is visible to them; our Processors.
- **Retention.** While your Account is active; removed on deletion per the Data Retention Policy (`/legal/data-retention`).
- **Your rights.** You control your profile content and may edit or remove it; plus the rights in §§42–48.

### 10. Identity Verification Data

- **Purpose.** To confirm that users are real people and support safety and integrity, where Identity Verification is used.
- **Data categories.** In Tirvea's systems we hold only the **outcome** of a check and an **opaque provider session reference** — we do **not** store copies of your identity document or verification selfie. The document and image handling occurs within the provider's environment.
- **Legal basis.** Performance of a contract and pre-contractual steps (Art. 6(1)(b)); compliance with legal obligations and fraud prevention (Art. 6(1)(c)/(f)); and consent where a check is optional (Art. 6(1)(a)). Detail is in the Identity Verification Policy (`/legal/identity-verification`).
- **Recipients.** Our identity-verification Processor, **Stripe Identity** (§36).
- **Retention.** Outcome and opaque references retained per the Data Retention Policy (`/legal/data-retention`); provider-side media handled under the provider's terms.
- **Your rights.** Access to outcomes, plus the rights in §§42–48.

### 11. Photo Verification Data

- **Purpose.** To confirm that the person using an Account matches the Account's profile photographs, reducing impersonation and fake profiles.
- **Data categories.** In Tirvea's systems we hold only an **opaque provider reference** and the verification outcome. We do **not** store copies of the liveness video or the profile-photo comparison images. The provider captures short liveness media to perform the check.
- **Legal basis.** Explicit consent for the associated biometric processing (Art. 9(2)(a)); and our legitimate interests in platform integrity for the non-biometric aspects (Art. 6(1)(f)). Detail is in the Photo Verification Policy (`/legal/photo-verification`).
- **Recipients.** Our verification Processors, **AWS Face Liveness** and **AWS Rekognition** (§35).
- **Retention.** Provider-held capture media is held briefly by the provider and then deleted; references and outcomes are retained per the Data Retention Policy (`/legal/data-retention`).
- **Your rights.** Withdraw consent at any time (§§28, 47), plus the rights in §§42–48.

### 12. Biometric Information

- **Purpose.** To perform Photo Verification (§11) — confirming you match your profile photographs. This is the **only** biometric processing on the Service.
- **Data categories.** A **face reference** in the form of an **opaque handle held by our provider** — **not** face geometry, images, video, or numeric similarity scores in Tirvea's systems, logs, or backups. Short liveness capture media exists only transiently in the provider's environment during the check.
- **Legal basis.** **Explicit consent under GDPR Article 9(2)(a)** for this Special Category Data. This is described in full in the Biometric Information Policy (`/legal/biometric-data`).
- **Recipients.** **AWS Face Liveness** and **AWS Rekognition** as Processors (§35); processing takes place in the EU.
- **Retention.** Provider-held capture media is deleted shortly after processing; the face reference is kept while your verification is valid, rotated periodically, and destroyed when you delete your account or withdraw consent. **[Legal review required before publication — exact retention and rotation periods pending completion of the DPIA.]**
- **Your rights.** Withdraw consent at any time via Settings › Privacy › Delete face data, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal; plus the rights in §§42–48. See the Biometric Information Policy (`/legal/biometric-data`).
