## 1. Output - Repository audit

Findings from the implementation (see Output 5 for the review):

**Implemented (repository-backed):**
- **Plans.** There are three plan tiers: a free tier (**Free**) and two paid plans, **Plus** and **Gold**. Both paid plans are billed **monthly** in **euro (EUR)**. Prices are configured (per Stripe price) and shown at checkout.
- **Billing provider.** Payments and subscriptions are handled by **Stripe**. Purchase is through **Stripe Checkout**; card details are held by Stripe, not by Tirvea.
- **Recurring billing and renewal.** Paid plans renew **monthly** by Stripe until cancelled.
- **Upgrades and downgrades.** Changing plan is an **in-place update of the single Stripe subscription** (never a second subscription), with a **prorated amount shown before you confirm** (a preview of the amount due, currency, and tax).
- **Cancellation and resume.** Cancellation takes effect at the **end of the current billing period** (cancel-at-period-end): you keep the plan until then, and you can **resume** before it ends. When Stripe ends the subscription, the plan reverts to Free.
- **Failed payments and grace.** If a renewal payment fails, the subscription enters a **past-due** state during which Stripe retries; a short **dunning grace window (about 7 days)** keeps the paid plan, after which the plan reverts to **Free**.
- **Subscription status.** Live statuses (active, trialing, past-due) map to the paid tier; other statuses (incomplete, unpaid, paused, canceled, expired) map to Free.
- **Trials.** The system supports a **trial** period (a trialing status is honoured as the paid tier). Whether a trial is offered depends on the plan configuration.
- **Taxes.** Tax (including VAT where applicable) is **calculated and collected by Stripe** and shown at checkout; Tirvea displays the tax amount when Stripe reports it.
- **Idempotency.** Duplicate checkout is prevented (an idempotency window), so a second checkout does not create a duplicate subscription.

**Not implemented / configuration-dependent (flagged):**
- **No coupons or promotional pricing** are implemented.
- **Only monthly billing** is implemented; there are no annual or other intervals.
- **Trial availability and tax configuration** depend on configuration; the specific trial terms and the tax jurisdictions covered are set by configuration and should be confirmed.

Each item is reflected in the relevant section and carries a **[Legal review required before publication.]** flag.

## 4. Output - Complete Subscription Terms

> **Reading note.** These Terms govern paid subscriptions (Plus and Gold), billed monthly by Stripe. Refunds and withdrawal are in the Refund Policy (`/legal/refund-policy`); the overall contract is the Terms of Service (`/legal/terms`).

### 1. Purpose

**Purpose.** To set out the terms on which WiseWave Limited provides paid subscriptions and premium features on Tirvea, including plans, billing, automatic renewal, upgrades, downgrades, and cancellation. These Terms form part of your agreement with WiseWave Limited (Terms of Service, `/legal/terms`).

### 2. Scope

**Purpose.** To state what these Terms cover. They cover the commercial relationship for paid subscriptions. They do not cover refunds or the right of withdrawal (Refund Policy, `/legal/refund-policy`), how your data is processed (Privacy Policy, `/legal/privacy`), or the general contract (Terms of Service, `/legal/terms`).

### 3. Definitions

**Purpose.** To define subscription terms. Terms defined in the Terms of Service (`/legal/terms`) keep their meaning.

- **"Plan" / "tier"** - a level of service: Free, Plus, or Gold.
- **"Subscription"** - a recurring paid plan (Plus or Gold).
- **"Billing period"** - the recurring interval for which you are charged (currently one month).
- **"Auto-renewal"** - automatic charging for the next billing period unless cancelled.
- **"Past due"** - a state where a renewal payment has failed and is being retried.

### 4. Subscription Plans

**Purpose.** To describe the plans. Tirvea offers a **Free** plan and two paid plans, **Plus** and **Gold**. Both paid plans are billed **monthly** in **euro (EUR)**. The features of each plan are described in the Service (§14) and may change (§15). Only monthly billing is currently offered; there are no annual plans. **[Legal review required before publication - confirm the plan line-up and that only monthly billing is offered.]**

### 5. Billing

**Purpose.** To explain how billing works. Payments and subscriptions are processed by **Stripe**. You purchase a subscription through **Stripe Checkout**, providing a payment method; your card details are held by Stripe, not by Tirvea (Privacy Policy §21, `/legal/privacy`). By subscribing, you authorise us and Stripe to charge your payment method for the plan you choose and its renewals. A subscription may include a **trial** where offered; where a trial applies, billing begins when the trial ends unless you cancel first. **[Legal review required before publication - confirm whether a trial is offered and its terms.]**

### 6. Auto-Renewal

**Purpose.** To explain renewal. Paid plans **renew automatically each month** until cancelled. Before you subscribe, you are shown the price and that the plan renews. You can cancel at any time to stop future renewals (§11); cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

### 7. Pricing

**Purpose.** To explain pricing. The price of each paid plan is **shown at checkout** before you confirm, in euro. Prices may change prospectively (§15); a change does not affect a billing period you have already paid for. For a plan change, the amount due (including any proration) is shown before you confirm (§9-§10).

### 8. Taxes

**Purpose.** To explain taxes. Tax, including **VAT where applicable**, is **calculated and collected by Stripe** and shown at checkout as part of the amount due. Whether a specific tax applies depends on your location and the applicable rules. **[Legal review required before publication - confirm the tax configuration and the jurisdictions in which tax is automatically calculated.]**

### 9. Upgrades

**Purpose.** To explain upgrades. You can upgrade (for example, from Plus to Gold). An upgrade is an **in-place change to your existing subscription**, not a second subscription. Before you confirm, you are shown the **amount due**, including any **proration** for the remainder of the current billing period, and any tax. The upgraded features apply once the change takes effect.

### 10. Downgrades

**Purpose.** To explain downgrades. You can downgrade (for example, from Gold to Plus). A downgrade is also an **in-place change to your existing subscription**, with the amount and any proration shown before you confirm. The lower-plan features apply as the change takes effect, per the amount shown.

### 11. Cancellation

**Purpose.** To explain cancellation. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the **end of the current billing period**: you keep your plan and its features until then, and you are not charged again unless you resume. You can **resume** a cancelled subscription before it ends. When the subscription ends, your plan reverts to **Free**.

### 12. Failed Payments

**Purpose.** To explain failed payments. If a renewal payment fails, your subscription becomes **past due** and Stripe **retries** the payment during a short **grace window (about 7 days)**, during which you keep your paid plan. If payment is not resolved within the grace window, your plan reverts to **Free**. You can update your payment method to restore the subscription.

### 13. Suspended Subscriptions

**Purpose.** To explain subscription suspension (distinct from account suspension). A subscription is effectively **suspended** while it is past due (§12) or where Stripe pauses it; during the grace window your paid features continue, and after it your plan reverts to Free. This is a **billing state and is different from account suspension** for policy breaches, which is governed by the Account Suspension Policy (`/legal/account-suspension`). If your account is suspended for a policy breach, your subscription and any refund are handled under that policy and the Refund Policy (`/legal/refund-policy`).

### 14. Premium Features

**Purpose.** To explain premium features. Paid plans (Plus and Gold) unlock **premium features** associated with each plan, as described in the Service. Premium features may be added, changed, or discontinued (§15). If your plan reverts to Free (cancellation, failed payment, or downgrade), premium features tied to a higher plan are no longer available. We do not remove features you have already paid for during a current paid period, save as permitted by law.

### 15. Changes

**Purpose.** To explain changes to plans, pricing, and features. We may add, change, or discontinue plans, prices, and premium features, and may run experimental or beta features. Price and plan changes are **prospective** and do not affect a billing period you have already paid for. Where required by law, we give you notice of a price change before it applies, and your statutory rights are preserved (§16). Changes are also subject to the Terms of Service (`/legal/terms`).

### 16. Consumer Rights

**Purpose.** To preserve your statutory rights. If you are a consumer, you have mandatory rights that these Terms do not exclude or limit, including rights relating to digital content and services, unfair contract terms, and, for distance contracts, a **right of withdrawal**. The right of withdrawal and refunds are set out in the **Refund Policy** (`/legal/refund-policy`) and Terms of Service §19-§22 (`/legal/terms`). Where any provision here conflicts with your statutory rights, your statutory rights prevail.

### 17. Liability

**Purpose.** To allocate liability. Liability arising from your subscription is governed by the limitation of liability in the Terms of Service §43 (`/legal/terms`), which does not exclude liability that cannot be excluded under mandatory law (including consumer law). These Terms do not create liability beyond, or reduce liability below, what that section and the law provide.

### 18. Contact

**Purpose.** To tell you how to reach us about your subscription.

- **Contracting entity:** WiseWave Limited (Company Number 762171)
- **Registered office:** 39 Cooley Park, Dundalk, Co. Louth, A91 AP2V, Ireland
- **Email:** info@tirvea.com

You can manage your subscription in the Service. For refunds and withdrawal, see the Refund Policy (`/legal/refund-policy`); for the overall contract, the Terms of Service (`/legal/terms`); for billing-data handling, the Privacy Policy (`/legal/privacy`).
